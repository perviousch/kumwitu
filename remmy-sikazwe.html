Remmy Sikazwe Football Tournament

Sports for Community Development

The Remmy Sikazwe Football Tournament uses sport as a tool for youth engagement, discipline, and community unity. This annual event brings together teams from across Mbala to compete, connect, and celebrate the power of sport in building stronger communities.

Tournament Goals

  • Promote unity and teamwork among youth
  • Develop discipline and leadership skills
  • Create opportunities for talent identification
  • Strengthen community bonds through shared experience

Tournament Impact

The Remmy Sikazwe Tournament has become a cornerstone event in Mbala, bringing together hundreds of youth, families, and community members each year. Beyond the competition, it fosters unity, discipline, and a sense of pride in our community.
