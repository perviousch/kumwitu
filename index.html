Kumwitu Uku Mwitu is a people-led movement rooted in our heritage, empowering youth and communities to build opportunity, unity, and shared prosperity.

Why Kumwitu Uku Mwitu?

Mbala is rich in culture, land, and talent. Yet many young people face limited access to opportunities, skills, and resources.

Kumwitu Uku Mwitu exists to bridge that gap by connecting youth to practical pathways for growth and dignity.
